How the Auction Works

Auction Timeline

1

24 Hours Before: Encryption Key Generated

Time-lock encryption key is created using drand, ensuring bids remain secret until reveal time.

2

Before 12:00 PM: Sellers List Assets

Sellers lock their assets with optional reserve prices. Listings are encrypted.

3

Before 12:30 PM: Buyers Submit Bids

Buyers submit encrypted bids with maximum price they're willing to pay per unit.

4

12:30 PM: Simultaneous Reveal

All encrypted bids and listings decrypt at once. No information cascades during submission.

5

After 12:30 PM: Sequential Clearing

Assets clear in order from smallest to biggest market (DOGE → ETH).

Price Discovery Mechanism

Uniform Clearing Price: All buyers pay the same price for an asset, determined by where supply meets demand.

Supply Curve: Built from sell orders sorted lowest to highest price.

Demand Curve: Built from buy orders sorted highest to lowest price.

Clearing: The price where cumulative demand equals or exceeds cumulative supply.

Sequential Clearing Order

Assets clear in predetermined order to prioritize liquidity for smaller markets:

DOGE → LINK → UNI → DOT → ATOM → AVAX → MATIC → SOL → BTC → ETH

Multiple Asset Bids

Capital Efficiency: Since all bids reveal simultaneously and you don't know which will win, you can bid on multiple assets to make your capital more efficient.

Budget Tracking: As auctions clear sequentially, your remaining budget decreases.

Forfeiture: If your budget runs out mid-clearing, remaining wins are forfeited.

Smart-Bid Fee

Over-Commitment Allowed: You can place bids totaling more than your balance.

Fee Structure: 5% fee on budget shortfall (total bids - balance) charged upfront.

Example: Balance = 100K LIBRA, Total Bids = 150K → Shortfall = 50K → Fee = 2.5K (5% of 50K)

Net Budget: 100K - 2.5K = 97.5K available for winning bids during sequential clearing.

Reserve Prices (Sellers)

Optional: Sellers can set minimum acceptable prices.

Fee Structure: Only charged if the asset doesn't sell (clearing price < reserve).

Calculation: Fee based on RMS delta between clearing and reserve prices.

Distribution: Fees distributed to buyers whose bids cleared successfully.
